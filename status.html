Our sensors are actively gathering air quality data from various locations in Nairobi. We continuously monitor their connectivity and functionality to ensure accurate and consistent measurements.

The density of sensors:

Policy Suggestions

Policies that could help reduce air pollution

Given that the transportation may be the most probable cause, the platform gives the following suggestions:

  • Using low emission vehicles for public transport
  • Setting the Ultra Low Emission Zone for the city centre
  • Restricting motor vehicles to the centre area during peak hours on weekdays according to the license plate of the vehicle.
